In January 2019, uPlexa Android Wallet was release. The wallet includes Node-O-Matic technology, allowing to scan for remote nodes, store, send, or receive uPlexa (UPX) with ease.

The uPlexa mobile wallet will allow everybody and anybody to get into the digital currency realm, allowing for simplistic trades, and lessening the high-fees for travelers and cross-border banking. Mobile wallets further help add an additional layer of privacy to those who want or need it, with options such as “Street Mode”, hiding all balances from any nosy individuals around you.

# Features

- **Create Wallets** - Create new wallet instantly.
- **Node-O-Matic** - Automatically scan available remote node.
- **Receive funds**
- **Send Funds**
- **QR Support** - Provide QR code to others to receive payments.
- **Fingerprint support** - Immediate unlock your wallet using fingerprint.
- **CrazzyPass** - Automatically check wallet password strength.
- **Street Mode** - Open your wallet without showing balances (prevent from surrounding onlookers).

# Installation & First Run

Donwload uPlexa Androit Wallet from Google Play Store and it will be installed automatically. Accept the permission required by the app and proceed with the node setting.
> **_Note:_** The permission required by the app are:
- **Camera** for the QR Code features.
- **Storage** to store and backup the walley key-file.

### Seting Up uPlexa Node
uPlexa Android Wallet required remote node to sync your wallet to the uPlexa blockchain. Add Node and enter the following details:
- **Hostname** - remote.uplexa.com
- **Port** - 21061
- **Node Name** - uPlexa (optional). For easy identification if you have your own private node.

**_Test_** the node connection and your wallet is ready to use.

# Create New Wallet

Create new wallet by click on " + " button and select **_Create new wallet_**.
<div class="video-responsive"><iframe src="https://drive.google.com/uc?id=1XrF6TmRPBCbwPkfZEf7lJsslwcoqnJ5O" width="1920" height="1080" allowFullScreen></iframe></div>

- **Wallet Name** - Name of your wallet. Try giving specific wallet name (i.e MainWallet, Mining, Exchanges, etc.)
- **Wallet passphrase** - Password for your wallet. Crazypass will automatically detect your wallet password strength.

Once you created your wallet, you will be directed to your wallet details which you have to make note or copied of your 25-word Mnemonic Seed.

| ❗ IMPORTANT   |
|----------------|

> Please copy your wallet **Mnemonic Seed**. Keeping this secure and private is very important, as it is the only way that you will be able to login to your account. As we don't store your wallet **Mnemonic Seed** on the server there is no way to recover it if it is lost! Your wallet **Mnemonic Seed** can also never be changed, and if it is stolen or otherwise compromised, you will have to move your funds to a new account with a new **Mnemonic Seed**. Therefore, it is best that you backup your wallet **Mnemonic Seed** by writing it down, perhaps obscuring it as part of a poem or letter, and storing it in multiple safe and secure places.

| ✅ SUCCESS |
|------------|
You have successfully create new uPlexa wallet. Now you can receive fund or start mining to earn uPlexa by referring to our guides: [uPlexa Android Miner](/uplexa_pow/1-android_mining/)


# Restore Wallet

Restore your wallet using 25-word Mnemonic Seed or Private-Keys. Recommended method is restore using 25-word Mnemonic Seed which is most common method for restoring cryptocurrency wallet.

<div class="video-responsive"><iframe src="https://drive.google.com/uc?id=1vBeChYAcvDI4hD-N9ZGkaXQ2PMrUjXDW" width="1920" height="1080" allowFullScreen></iframe></div>

- **Wallet Name** - Name of your wallet. Try giving specific wallet name (i.e MainWallet, Mining, Exchanges, etc.)
- **Wallet passphrase** - Password for your wallet. Crazypass will automatically detect your wallet password strength.
- **25-word Mnemonic Seed** - Your wallet specific seed word.
- **Blockheight or Date (YYY/MM/DD)** - Restore blockheight. If you remember blockheight or date you wallet is first created, you may enter the information accordingly or refer to uPlexa blockchain summary here: [uPlexa Blockchain](/technical_data/#blockchain). Alternatively, you may set the blockheight to "0" to scan from the beginning of the blockchain.


# Scan Wallet
If you just restore your wallet, you wallet will need to scan the uPlexa blockchain from your wallet creation date/blockheight. Please take note that it may take a while to scan the blockchain (~ 5-10min). This a one time process everytime you restore your wallet. Once it completed, your wallet will automatically scan the remote node and update your wallet to the latest blockheight.
